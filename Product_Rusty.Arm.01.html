RUSTY ARM 01

A 3-DOF robotic arm controlled by dual joysticks for precise, smooth, and real-time movement.

Overview

RUSTY ARM 01 is a compact 3-servo robotic arm designed for precision and flexibility. It uses two analog joysticks to control three servo joints, allowing smooth, coordinated movement without automatic resets. Built for learning and experimentation, it demonstrates real-world robotics control and motion accuracy through a simple yet powerful interface.
